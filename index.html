About Me

My name is Talal Bukhowa.

I'm Bahraini and am currently 23 years old.

I'm currently a student at the British University of Bahrain, majoring in Software Engineering. ‎

My history

I've been studying in the USA for three years and have spent two of those years in the state of Arizona

I have been fortunate to explore the areas around Arizona and take pictures of my experiences during my stay there.

The pictures provided on this website are all taken in AZ at different locations. ‎

Life in Arizona

Arizona is a desert state, therefore the atmosphere there is not much different than Bahrain.

The people in Arizona are very friendly and helpful, making life there as a college student a great experience which I highly recommend anyone to have.

Since Arizona is a big state, there is a lot you can do there in terms of exploring and sightseeing.
