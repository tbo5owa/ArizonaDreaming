location

Wonder Spaces is located in the Scottsdale Fashion Square, Scottsdale, Arizona.

Being located in a mall makes it easy to find parking to enter the art show.
‎

Where it gets its name from

Wonder Spaces is an art exhibit that changes year-round, playing with visual illusions and art structures.

Every area of the exhibit has a different theme to it, while one area is dark and filled with small lights, the other is a lit room with art pieces on the wall.

Since the art in the exhibit changes frequently, you're left to wonder what you'll find next time.
‎

Popularity

Wonder Spaces is mostly popular amongst teens and young adults since the fresh artwork are perfect for picture taking.

A lot of their visitors are students of Arizona State University. However, highschool students from around Tempe and Scottsdale also visit the art exhibit frequently.

Tickets could be bought online or upon entry, although getting the tickets online would be best for a faster entry.
‎

Scenery

The art exhibit features art pieces from multiple artists.

Every room offers a different perspective from the artist's point of view, some being visual illusions while others are large structures.

It's interesting to see how an artist visualizes art and can share that vision with others.

From flashing lights to mind-bending sculptures, Wonder Spaces offers a change of scenery to all its visitors.
‎

The experience

With a lot of rooms to visit, it's easy to lose track of time while looking at art.

The first room I visited had a box that you enter into, the walls inside were black and the floor was a mirror, with projectors on top shining visually pleasing light patterns that lit up the whole room.

The second room had string lights hung from the ceiling flashing with patterns that were very interesting to look at.

Every part of the exhibit left you with a yearning for more, with the last room being an art piece that visitors create by leaving written notes on the wall.
